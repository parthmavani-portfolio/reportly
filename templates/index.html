What you get

Built for FMCG analysts

6-Theme Analysis

Volume & Distribution, Raw Material, Brand & Innovation, Margin, Growth & Strategy, Risk & Headwinds — with sentence-level evidence.

Sentiment Scoring

VADER compound sentiment per section, chunked for accuracy on long text. Each section classified as Positive, Neutral, or Negative.

Management Guidance

Automatically extracts forward-looking statements on revenue growth, margin targets, capex plans, and market expansion strategies.

Text-Derived PMI

A Purchase Manager Index computed from report language — New Orders, Output, Employment, Supplier Deliveries, and Inventories.

Narrative Classification

Classifies the overall report into one of six archetypes — from Aggressive Growth to Defensive Stress — based on theme ratios and sentiment.

PDF Dashboard + Excel

A polished multi-page PDF with embedded charts, company logo, and full source references — plus a detailed Excel workbook with raw data.
